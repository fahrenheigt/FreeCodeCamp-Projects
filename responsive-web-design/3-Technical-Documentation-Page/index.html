Welcome to
League of Legends

League of Legends (LoL) is a fast-paced, competitive online game developed by Riot Games. Since its release in 2009, it has become one of the most popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) games in the world, offering intense strategy, teamplay, and action-packed combat.

In League of Legends, players assume the role of champions—powerful characters with unique abilities—working together in teams of five to destroy the opposing team’s Nexus, a critical structure located in their base. The game is known for its depth, where mastering champions, team strategies, and map awareness are crucial to success.

What sets League of Legends apart is its diverse roster of over 160 champions, each with distinct playstyles, strengths, and weaknesses. Whether you prefer the role of a damage-dealing assassin, a resilient tank, or a masterful support, there’s a champion for every player.

Join the millions of players worldwide who are part of this dynamic and ever-evolving universe. Whether you're looking to climb the ranked ladder, play casually with friends, or explore the lore behind the champions, League of Legends offers an experience that’s both challenging and incredibly rewarding.
